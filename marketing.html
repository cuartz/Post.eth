PostEth blends blockchain with geolocation to build real communities around you. Each ZiPoint is a hub where you can post, chat and view ephemeral stories.

Signing in with your Ethereum Name Service domain proves your identity. Every interaction earns reputation, helping you validate content and unlock perks.
